NJPW Road To Destruction Results (9/22): SANADA, Tetsuya Naito, More

July 27, 2024
blog

On September 22, New Japan Pro Wrestling continued their NJPW Road to Destruction tour with the eleventh night of action from Texport Imabari in Imabari, Ehime, Japan.

SANADA, Tetsuya Naito, Will Ospreay, and other stars competed.

The results, courtesy of Fightful, are as follows:

  • YOH def. Yuto Nakashima
  • Great Bash Heel (Togi Makabe & Tomoaki Honma) def. Satoshi Kojima & Oskar Leube
  • Shota Umino, Yuji Nagata & Master Wato def. Strong Style (Ren Narita & El Desperado) & Tiger Mask
  • Be-Bop Tag Team (Toru Yano & Hiroshi Tanahashi) def. Ryusuke Taguchi & Oleg Boltin
  • CHAOS (Kazuchika Okada, Tomohiro Ishii, Hirooki Goto & YOSHI-HASHI) def. TMDK (Zack Sabre Jr., Shane Haste, Bad Dude Tito & Mikey Nicholls)
  • Los Ingobernables de Japon (BUSHI, Hiromu Takahashi, Shingo Takagi, Tetsuya Naito & Yota Tsuji) def. United Empire (HENARE, Jeff Cobb, Will Ospreay, Callum Newman & Great-O-Khan)
  • House Of Torture (SHO, EVIL, Dick Togo & Yujiro Takahashi) def. Just 5 Guys (SANADA, Taichi, DOUKI & TAKA Michinoku)

SANADA will compete at DEFY Genesis. More information is available here.
