Lucy Walters Bio (Age)

Lucy Walters has not made her exact birth date public knowledge but it is no secret that the English actress from Oxfordshire was born on the 20th of May. More so, details regarding her parents, early life, and education have not been made public. But in an interview, Walters once revealed that her theatrical inclinations began from her childhood days. Given her love for storytelling, she joined the Houston Grand Opera as a kid. She also learned to play the piano as a child and once nurtured dreams of progressing to musical theatre.

To further hone her skills, Walters studied theatre in college at the University of Texas, Austin. Her decision to switch to acting came as an afterthought following her relocation to New York a few years ago. This was because she was awakened to the realization of the power that comes with good plays and films. Today, the rest is history as Walter feels at peace with her choice of career.

Walters’ versatile talents can be seen in several hit movies and TV shows portraying different characters. Notably, some of her renowned movie features include Shame (2011), The Brass Teapot (2012), Bastards of Young (2013), B-Side (2013), Lies I Told My Little Sister (2014), Here Alone (2016), and many more. She also has various TV appearances to her credit such as Blue Bloods, The Good Wife, Power, Gossip Girl, Army Wives, Get Shorty, and Falling Water, among others.

For now, Lucy seems to be solely focused on her career. Though she sometimes has complicated feelings about her job, she remains steadfast nurturing hopes of landing bigger and more challenging roles in big budget movies.

Is She Married? Who Is Her Husband Or Boyfriend?

In fact, Walters further stoked the speculations when she made an Instagram post in 2018 sending birthday felicitations to her onscreen daughter. So far, Lucy Walters has succeeded in keeping details of her personal life under wraps leaving the public with just speculations.
