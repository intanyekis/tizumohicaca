Chris Brown Dismisses Quavo's &quot;Google Raps&quot; Diss, Declines To Respond

August 13, 2024
blog

Chris Brown ain’t too impressed with Quavo‘s latest diss response. On Monday (April 22), Brown reacted to Qua’s track, “Over H*es & Bi**hes.” 

In the venomous cut, the Migos rapper used unreleased Takeoff vocals as he threatened physical violence against Brown. The R&B singer shrugged off the bars and took to his Instagram Story to get his jokes off. “[face palm and laughing emojis] Google raps THAT SH*T IS POOOOOOOH,” he typed in response. “Damn, and I was excited…that sh*t don’t even need a response. Takeoff rap better.” 

He followed up with several tomato emojis with a simple message: “C’mon Quavious.” Brown’s subsequent posts included a seemingly A.I.-crafted picture of Quavo crying with his diss song, “Weakest Link,” sprawled across his forehead. The Virginia native also included a Spongebob Squarepants meme, asserting that Quavo’s raps “stink.” 

Chris Brown also honed in on one bar that had him dying of laughter. “Why they got Quavo out here going back and forth with a singer that turned to a junkie?/Never got light skin versus brown skin, ni**a finna divide the country,” Quavo rapped. Brown shared a message with an associate as they were befuddled by the lyrics.

Elsewhere in the song, Quavo responded to the “Wall to Wall” artist’s claims that he had intercourse with Saweetie. Qua also addressed the multihyphenate bringing up Takeoff in his diss song, “Weakest Link.” He also took a shot at Brown’s alleged drug abuse and claimed his track was trash. 

“Lil’ boy wanna die ’bout some c**chie? You still f**ked up ’bout Karrueche?/You tried to beat up Teyana, but Usher wouldn’t let you do it/The cocaine got ’em, your honor, bipolar disorder, no wonder/You was the greatest, ni**a, you fumbled, going out sad, I’m watching you crumble,” he rapped. 

“Who gassed you up to put that out?/Yeah, that was sh*tty, ni**a /I can take a model bi**h and make a Saweetie, ni**a /Crackhead Michael Jackson, bi**h, you better beat it, ni**a/I’m still f**ked up about Takeoff, I want who did it, ni**a.”
