Biography

Arthel Neville was born in New Orleans, LA on October 20, 1962. Known best for hosting such Fox News programs as Fox Files and Celebrity Spotlight, this broadcast journalist also led the network’s coverage of the aftermath of Hurricane Katrina in her hometown of New Orleans, Louisiana. She and broadcast legend Dick Clark once co-hosted the Miss Teen USA beauty pageant. After briefly acting on the NBC soap opera Days of Our Lives, she studied journalism at Southern Methodist University and the University of Texas at Austin. While a student at the latter school, she became the first African American woman reporter to work for Texas’ KVUE-TV ABC affiliate.
