What did Carson Daly do before 'Today' gig?

Carson Daly's career journey began in the radio industry, where he worked for stations in San Jose and California.

During this early phase, he worked as a radio DJ at the College of the Desert, earning the nickname "Kid Carson." He also interned for Jimmy Kimmel, a longtime acquaintance, who was hosting a radio show in Palm Springs at the time.

His talent caught the attention of MTV, and he was recruited to be a VJ for their show ‘Motel California’.

MTV was so impressed with Daly that they hired him full-time, requiring him to relocate to New York and host ‘MTV Live’. His tenure at MTV also included hosting ‘TRL’ from 1998 to 2003, where he introduced the top ten music videos and interviewed celebrity guests.

In 2002, Daly transitioned to NBC, becoming the host of ‘Last Call with Carson Daly.’ He expanded his role at the network, taking on the role of host for NBC's New Year's Eve special in 2003.

In 2011, Daly's career took a new turn as he became an executive producer and host for NBC's reality music competition, ‘The Voice.’

Over time, he became the sole host of the show. In 2013, Daly added the role of social media correspondent for the ‘Today’ show to his list of accomplishments.

Beyond television hosting, he also made cameo appearances in shows like ‘My Name is Earl’ and ‘Chappelle's Show.’

Daly's diverse career has spanned radio, television, and even executive production, solidifying his presence in the entertainment industry.

How many houses does Carson Daly have?

Carson Daly has made some significant real estate investments throughout his career.

In 2013, he purchased a substantial home on Long Island for $6.5 million, indicating his commitment to upscale living.

Additionally, in Santa Monica, California, Daly owns a substantial property that includes two adjacent houses.

While they may appear as separate residences from the street, Daly reportedly combined their backyards to create an expansive outdoor space featuring a lawn and a pool area.

In total, Carson Daly's real estate portfolio boasts an estimated value of around $20 million, reflecting his taste for luxurious properties.

Who is Carson Daly's wife?

Carson Daly met his now-wife, Siri Daly nee Pinter on his show 'Last Call With Carson Daly' where she worked as the writer’s assistant, according to People. 

The couple got engaged in 2013 but finally got married right before Christmas in 2015. Siri, who runs a food blog called 'Siriously Delicious' and authored several cookbooks, is currently a food correspondent for 'Today'. 

 The couple have four children together. 

Their first three children, son Jackson James and daughters Etta, and London, were born before they got married. Their youngest, Goldie, was born during the pandemic, in March 2020.
