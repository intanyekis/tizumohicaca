Beverly Elaine Aadland Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Beverly Elaine Aadland (September 16, 1942 – January 5, 2010) was an American film actress.She appeared in films including South Pacific. As a teenager, she co-starred in the Errol Flynn film Cuban Rebel Girls, and later entered a relationship with him. She and Flynn were considered for the lead roles in Stanley Kubrick's Lolita, but after allegations of statutory rape were made against Flynn due to her age, the parts went to Sue Lyon and James Mason instead. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
