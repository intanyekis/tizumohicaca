Harrison Ford Admitted He Could've Been A Better Family Man Before His Divorce

August 22, 2024
blog

Highlights

  • Harrison Ford admitted to being a flawed parent with major regrets about his relationships with kids from past marriages.
  • In contrast, Ford shares a strong bond with his adopted son Liam Flockhart.
  • Ford's dedication to improving relationships is evident in how he adapts to support his family.

Between his roles in the Star Wars and Indiana Jones movies, Harrison Ford starred in a slew of blockbusters that made a fortune at the box office. As a result, Ford has been able to earn a huge fortune and become richer than his former Star Wars co-stars like Mark Hamill.

Given everything Ford has accomplished, it may seem like he has it all. As it turns out, however, Ford has major regrets about one part of his life. According to what Ford once revealed, he regrets the kind of parent he was to his kids from his divorces.

This article will reveal how much Harrison Ford regrets his relationship with his children from his first two marriages. The article will then look at Ford's relationship with his adopted son from his marriage to Calista Flockhart.

Harrison Ford's Relationships With His Kids From His Divorces Were Affected By His Success

Most of the time when celebrities get interviewed, they do whatever they can to look good. When Harrison Ford was interviewed by Esquire in 2023, however, the famed Star Wars and Indiana Jones actor was honest about who he was. Most notably, Ford admitted that he should have been a better father to his kids from his divorces.

During their conversation, Esquire's interviewer and Ford discussed what kind of parent the famed actor is. Amazingly enough, Ford admitted that he wasn't the best parent because he was too self-involved.

"I accept my flaws and my failures—I don’t accept them, I own them. And certainly the more constant gardener is the better parent, and I’ve been out of town, up my own ass, for most of my life.”

While Harrison Ford said he wasn't the best father, his son Ben Ford once paid tribute to him. In a touching 2019 Father's Day post about his famous father, Ben Ford started by writing about how his famous father allowed him to find his own way in the world.

My dad or pops (newly adapted) has always had this way of giving me the room to grow, never pushing doors open for me but rather gently showing me the door instead.

Following that, Ben Ford explained what his father Harrison Ford taught him.

"He taught me resiliency as a young father and later helped give me the courage to pursue my dreams. His craftsman approach and his pragmatic view of life are qualities I have tried to emulate throughout my life and kept me on level ground while navigating life’s challenges and celebrating my successes. You are the best….Happy Father’s Day!"

Harrison Ford Loves The Youngest Member Of His Family, His Adopted Son Liam Flockhart Ford

Roughly a year before Harrison Ford met his wife, Calista Flockhart, she adopted her son. After Ford and Flockhart got married, he adopted the son they now share, Liam Flockhart Ford.

While speaking to Parade in 2010, Ford revealed when he met his youngest son and the role they have in each other's lives. "I wasn't expecting it at all. The unexpected part was certainly true and the joy part is also true. I think Liam was about 6 or 8 months old when I met him and Calista. We have been together ever since."

Ford then went on to discuss his relationship with his youngest son and his wife. Based on what Ford had to say during that interview, he has become less self-involved over time as he now does whatever his son and wife want him to do on the weekends.

"On the weekends, I do whatever Calista and Liam want to do. We'll take a couple of hours on Sunday morning to go motorcycle riding or go for a hike."

Many years later during an appearance on The Ellen DeGeneres Show in 2019, Ford gave a major insight into his relationship with Liam Flockhart Ford. While talking to DeGeneres, Ford revealed that he went skydiving for the first time in his late 70s because his youngest son wanted him to.

In 2020, Liam Flockhart Ford began his college career in style thanks to his famous father. According to a report from People, Harrison Ford flew his youngest son to college in a private plane the actor piloted.

A couple of years later in 2021, Ford, Flockhart, and their adopted son were photographed on vacation in Croatia. From all accounts, Ford and Liam Flockhart Ford have a tight relationship.
