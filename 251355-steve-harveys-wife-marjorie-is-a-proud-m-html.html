Steve Harvey's Wife Is a Proud Mom Posing in Front of Lori's Billboard Ad for Naked Wardrobe

June 15, 2024
blog

Marjorie Harvey, Steve Harvey's wife, expressed a proud moment for Lori’s great achievement this year. See how the doting mom felt when she saw the billboard ad.

Lori Harvey started 2021 strong - she found love in actor Michael B. Jordan and now she has a clothing collection. Her mom, Marjorie Harvey, is beyond happy with her accomplishments.

The 56-year-old proud mom, who is the wife of "Family Feud" show host Steve Harvey, recently took to her Instagram Story to share how elated she was in seeing Lori's fruit of labor.

In her photo, Marjorie posed on the sidewalk in front of a black car. She stopped by the area because she found Lori's large billboard ad that read, "LH x Naked Wardrobe."

"Proud Mama @loriharvey"

A BODY-INCLUSIVE COLLECTION

The social media star previously said in an interview that she created her loungewear pieces to motivate women to feel comfortable and confident with their own skin.

Lori's collection took a few months to create. Her loungewear line includes jogger pants and crop tops in various neutral colors such as black, white, mocha, and graphite.

A lover of sweats, Lori added that she focused on loungewear pieces because it is comfortable and versatile. A person can wear her pieces stylishly on any occasion.

An Instagram Story screenshot of Marjorie Harvey taking a snap of Lori Harvey's billboard ad. | Photo: instagram.com/marjorie_harvey

A VALID REPRESENTATION

While she is not the first Black woman in the modeling industry, Lori is the first woman with a height of five-foot-three to grace the runway. She is shorter than an average model.

The former champion equestrian debuted her catwalk under the Dolce & Gabbana label, which earned her a ticket to grace more runway shows from various brands.

Lori didn't just shake up the fashion world; she surprised her fans when she and Michael became Instagram official.

Though she didn't have the same model qualities as Kendall Jenner or Gigi Hadid, Lori shared that she is proud of not being considered as a traditional model.

Being a unique model, Lori explained that it had opened many doors for a new generation of models that look like her. She believes that non-traditional models are changing the fashion industry.

Lori didn't just shake up the fashion world; she surprised her fans when she and Michael became Instagram official. The couple announced on Instagram on January 10 that they are in a relationship.

Before the news, many fans speculated in November that she and the "Black Panther" star were dating. With their publicized relationship, Steve said that he's keeping an eye on Michael.
