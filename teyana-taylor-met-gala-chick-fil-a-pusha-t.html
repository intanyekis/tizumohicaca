Teyana Taylor 'Embarrasses' Pusha T With Met Gala Food Order

June 02, 2024
blog

Pusha T wasn’t feeling Teyana Taylor‘s choice of dinner at the 2023 Met Gala as the singer had some Chick-fil-A delivered to the prestigious event.

The moment went down in New York City on Monday (May 1), with Pusha calling out his former G.O.O.D. Music labelmate on his Instagram Stories for “embarrassing” him in front of all his celebrity friends.

“Teyana Taylor’s embarrassing me. She brought [Chick-fil-A] to the #MetGala. I want her away from me!…#HARLEM,” King Push wrote.

He then added that Usher tried to intervene and stop the meal from transpiring, but that Teyana went ahead and devoured the fast food anyway.

Teyana Taylor is hardly the first celeb to enjoy some fast food in a swanky setting. Ray J went viral last year after he was seen devouring a Nissin Cup Noodles during the 2022 BET Awards.

Despite the roasting he received online, the “Sexy Can I” singer later revealed that because of the viral moment, he had closed a business deal with the noodles company.

AD LOADING...

“Just got off the phone with Cup Noodles,” Ray J said. “Shit good.”

The 2023 Met Gala was filled with many memorable moments. The night’s theme, “A Line of Beauty,” honored the late German designer Karl Lagerfeld, who was known for his celebrated work at Chanel and who died in 2019 at age 85.

AD LOADING...

Other notable attendees of the night included Rihanna and A$AP Rocky, Kendrick Lamar, Dr. Dre, Diddy and Yung Miami, and Ice Spice, who made her Met Gala debut after receiving a special invite from Vogue editor-in-chief Anna Wintour.

Doja Cat also turned heads at the gala thanks to her intense feline look, which was meant to embody Lagerfeld’s famous cat Choupette.

As for Teyana Taylor, she recently expressed regret over how little Pharrell pushed for her success as a teen star while she was signed to him early on in her career.

The R&B singer inked a deal with P’s Star Trak imprint back in 2007 when she was just 15 years old, before later being let go in 2012. Taylor then joined up with Kanye West’s G.O.O.D. Music.

AD LOADING...

“Getting signed to a man, it was like a figure to me … I looked at these people like figures. Whether it was as a dad, as a brother, it didn’t matter,” she said in a recent interview with Angie Martinez’ IRL podcast.

“So it’s like, you didn’t see me, you didn’t hear me, you didn’t protect me. you kind of just like fed me to the wolves type-shit without probably even knowing,” she told Martinez. “Some of those conversations still need to be had.”

AD LOADING...

However, she said that it’s nothing but love for the Neptunes hitmaker.

“It was clearly an emotional space for the both of us and my mom always told me to be grounded and just be thankful for people that are blessings to me,” Taylor said.

“It didn’t matter that I was hurt, I was a mom, so I had learned patience I learned grace, so I was really hitting him up to just say thank you … Because knowing him as well as I know him it wasn’t anything you did on purpose.”
