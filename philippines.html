The soaring remittance-driven economy has significantly increased the wealth of Philippines’ richest people by $13 billion to $47.4 in 2011-2012. According to Forbes, it has outperformed many of its neighboring countries in Asia. The Philippines now has total of 15 billionaires from 11 as of last year due to outsourcing, tourism, ongoing consumer boom, 17% increase in stock market plus the 6.4% economic growth during the last quarter.

Henry Sy is still the richest person in the country with a total net worth of $9.1 billion which increased by 1.9 billion last year according to Forbes magazine. Enrique Razon Jr. is this year’s highest gainer in the Philippines. His wealth increased by $2 billion to $3.6 billion making him the third richest person in the country.
