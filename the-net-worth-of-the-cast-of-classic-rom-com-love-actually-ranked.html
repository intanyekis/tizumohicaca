Love Actually, a British romantic comedy released in 2003, has captured the hearts of millions with its intertwining stories of love, heartbreak, and the magic of the holiday season. Directed by Richard Curtis, the film boasts an ensemble cast of talented actors who bring their characters to life, creating a memorable cinematic experience. The movie's premise revolves around ten stories, all connected somehow, showcasing the various forms of love in our lives. Love Actually was a box-office success, grossing over $246 million worldwide and solidifying its place as a holiday classic.

The film's star-studded cast delivered outstanding performances and achieved great success in their careers. With diverse acting styles and backgrounds, these actors have amassed impressive net worths over the years.

Bill Nighy - $8 Million

Bill Nighy portrayed the aging rock star Billy Mack in Love Actually, whose quest for a Christmas number-one hit adds a touch of humor to the film. With a net worth of $8 million, Nighy has enjoyed a successful film, television, and theater career, earning numerous awards and nominations for his performances. His other notable roles include Davy Jones in the Pirates of the Caribbean franchise, Viktor in the Underworld series, and Minister Rufus Scrimgeour in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1.

Laura Linney - $10 Million

Laura Linney, who played Sarah, a woman torn between her love for her coworker and her devotion to her mentally ill brother, has a net worth of $10 million, as per The Things. Linney's nuanced performance in Love Actually is just one example of her extensive acting career, which includes roles in critically acclaimed films such as You Can Count on Me, Mystic River, and The Savages. She has also starred in the successful television series The Big C and Ozark, earning multiple Emmy Awards for her work.

Martine McCutcheon - $13 Million

Martine McCutcheon, known for her role as Natalie, the charming and down-to-earth assistant to the Prime Minister, has a net worth of $13 million. McCutcheon's portrayal of Natalie won the hearts of audiences, and her career has spanned acting, singing, and television presenting. Before Love Actually, she gained fame as Tiffany Mitchell in the popular British soap opera EastEnders. As a singer, she has released several albums, with her debut single Perfect Moment reaching the top of the UK charts as per IMDB.

Martin Freeman - $20 Million

In Love Actually, Martin Freeman played John, a stand-in actor who falls in love with his fellow stand-in, Judy. With a net worth of $20 million, Freeman has become an acclaimed name in the industry, starring in popular films and television series such as The Hobbit, where he portrayed Bilbo Baggins, and Sherlock, in which he played Dr. John Watson. Freeman has also appeared in the Marvel Cinematic Universe as Everett K. Ross, further cementing his status as a versatile and accomplished actor.

Colin Firth - $25 Million

Colin Firth portrayed the lovelorn writer Jamie in Love Actually. Firth's performance in the film is just one of many in his illustrious career, which includes an Academy Award win for his role in The King's Speech. He has also starred in successful films such as Bridget Jones's Diary, A Single Man, and the Kingsman series, showcasing his ability to excel in dramatic and comedic roles. Firth's exceptional movie career has led to his impressive $25 Million net worth, as per Wealthy Gorilla.

Emma Thompson - $40 Million

Emma Thompson, who played Karen, a woman grappling with her husband's infidelity, has a stunning net worth of $40 million. Thompson's powerful performance in Love Actually is a testament to her acting prowess, earning her numerous accolades throughout her career. She has won Academy Awards for her roles in Howards End and Sense and Sensibility. She has also appeared in the Harry Potter series as Professor Sybill Trelawney, demonstrating her versatility as an actress.

Keira Knightley - $80 Million

Keira Knightley, who starred as the newlywed Juliet in Love Actually, has an impressive net worth of $80 million as reported by The Wikifeed. Knightley's role in the film marked the beginning of a successful career, with starring roles in blockbuster films such as Pirates of the Caribbean, where she played Elizabeth Swann, and Pride & Prejudice, in which she portrayed Elizabeth Bennet. Knightley has also appeared in critically acclaimed films like Atonement and The Imitation Game, earning multiple award nominations for her performances.

Liam Neeson - $145 Million

Liam Neeson, who played the widowed father Daniel in Love Actually, has a staggering net worth of $145 million, making him the second richest in this list. Neeson's heartfelt performance in the film is just one of many in his extensive career, which includes action-packed roles in films like Taken, where he played the iconic character Bryan Mills, and Schindler's List, in which he portrayed Oskar Schindler. Neeson's ability to take on a wide range of roles has made him one of Hollywood's most respected and successful actors.

Hugh Grant - $150 Million

Topping the list with a net worth of $150 million is Hugh Grant, who portrayed the charismatic Prime Minister, David, in Love Actually. Grant's performance in the film is a prime example of his charm and wit, making him a leading man in romantic comedies for decades. His other notable films include Four Weddings and a Funeral, Notting Hill, and Bridget Jones's Diary. Grant has also taken on more dramatic roles in recent years, such as his critically acclaimed performance in the HBO miniseries The Undoing.

Love Actually cast members have contributed to the film's enduring appeal and achieved remarkable success in their careers. Their collective talent and charisma have left an indelible mark on the entertainment industry, and their performances in Love Actually will continue to be cherished by audiences for years.
