Rapper Blac Youngsta Brother Died in A Shooting in Memphis

September 03, 2024
blog

The city of Memphis is filled with sadness today due to the sudden loss of a local hip-hop artist, Blac Youngsta’s brother who tragically lost his life after being shot near a B.P. gas station in South Memphis yesterday morning. The community is now grappling with a heavy cloud of grief as they mourn this untimely and devastating event.

Rapper Blac Youngsta Brother Died: How It Happened

On the morning of August 18th, at approximately 9:30 a.m., a distressing incident unfolded near a Valero B.P. gas station close to South Side Park. People who were present at the scene reported hearing several loud gunshots coming from a passing vehicle. Amidst the chaos, one of the bullets found its target, striking a young man. Witnesses later revealed that this young man was the brother of Blac Youngsta, a well-known rapper from Memphis.

Prompt medical assistance was summoned, and the injured young man was swiftly taken to Regional One Hospital. Despite the efforts of the medical team, tragically, his life could not be saved. Upon arrival at the hospital, he was pronounced deceased, leaving his loved ones utterly shattered by the devastating loss of yet another young life. The incident has left a profound impact on the community as they grapple with the sorrow and heartache caused by this unfortunate event.

The MPD (Memphis Police Department) has requested that anyone who knows anything about the shooting get in touch with CrimeStoppers at 901-528-2274. If you have any information, please don’t hesitate to reach out and ask for help.
