Harold Hogue (Ice Train) Passes Away

July 15, 2024
blog

Former WCW star Ice Train has passed away.

Marc Mero and Diamond Dallas Page both took to social media to share their thoughts as they announced that Harold Hogue, also known as Ice Train in WCW, had passed away. Mero called him one of the good guys and said he was an amazing friend. Page highlighted his lengthy friendship with Hogue and emphasized his kindness.

As Ice Train, Hogue had a memorable run in WCW. He competed at the first Fall Brawl event, and he had an undefeated streak. At different points of his time with the company he teamed up with Ice, and he was part of The New Blood. He came out of retirement for a one-off match in 2019.

WrestleZone sends our condolences to the friends, family, and fans of Harold Hogue.
