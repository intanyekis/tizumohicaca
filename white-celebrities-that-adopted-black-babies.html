Sandra and Son,Louis Bardi Bullock

“The first time I met Louis, it was like the whole world just got quiet. He was so small, so still. All the trivial things that I had allowed to take up so much of my time just didn’t have room in life anymore . All I said when i met him was, ‘hey, there you are.’ It was like he had always been a part of our lives. Life just shifted to be about Louis and what was needed for him.  No hiccup. It just fit, and I never had another moment of worry again,” Sandra says of her son Louis Bardo Bullock.
