True Story Behind FNAF

The Game Franchise was inspired from the incident at Chuck E. Cheese. 5 workers including the Manager of Chuck E. Cheese stayed late at their work that day. Out of the 5 workers, 3 were teenagers who were cleaning the table, another one was cleaning at the Kitchen and the remaining one person was the Manager of the Chuck E. Cheese. Apart from them, a 19- year old male hid in the bathroom that day. He shot and  killed all the 3 teen workers who were cleaning the tables and shot the worker at the Kitchen and made the manager open the safe and shot her. He killed them because of being fired from his job after having a disagreement.  He was caught and sentenced to death. Each Foxy, Freddy, Chica, Bonnie, and Golden Freddy represent the life of the lost people.
