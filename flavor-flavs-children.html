Let’s Meet Flavor Flav’s 8 Children

His Three Children with Karren Ross

He fathered three children with Karren Ross named, Shaniq Drayton, Karren Drayton, and Kayla Drayton. When they were still little, their father was absent from their lives as he was dealing with personal issues ranging from drug use to many clashes with the law.

In 1991, he was involved in a domestic violence case against Ross which he pled guilty. Following this, he served 30 days in custody. The relationship never reached the marriage stage and they parted ways.

Since it ended, both have kept the details away from the media light. Also, their three children live quietly. Nonetheless, we gathered that Kayla started her educational pursuit at Albany Leadership Charter High School for Girls and welcomed her child in 2023. With regards to Karren, she is married to long-time boyfriend Michael R. Derosa whom she shares a child with.

Who is the Rapper’s Wife?

As you would easily find, the Public Enemy hypeman had appeared on Flavor of Love, yet, when it came time to marry, he chose his 7th child’s mother, Trujillo, over other women that appeared on the show.

A closer look at her life shows that she is also a reality star who likes to maintain a quiet life making it hard to know the details about her background. As it is, even her birth year is not verified by the celebrity wife leading to the speculation that she was born in 1975.

During their marriage, the Hall of Famer had a close call with the law in 2012. Reportedly, he was arrested for assaulting Trujillo and her son, Gibran Trujillo from another relationship. He allegedly threatened to kill Gibran when he took a kitchen knife and went after him. Yet after his arrest, he refuted any claims that he tried to kill Trujillo’s son.

Despite this, they tried to make the relationship work by going to therapy. They appeared on Couples Therapy, a reality series that showcases couples receiving therapy sessions. Their session proved quite vital as their marriage withstood the challenges and they forged ahead.

Moving forward, Trujillo collapsed during the shooting of Couples Therapy in April 2013. This led to speculations that it was a result of drug use and overdose of painkillers. However, it was later reported and linked to stress and an accidental overdose of painkillers. It would be recalled that he had his last child with another woman while still married to her, yet nothing has been heard about their parting ways.

At the moment the status of their marriage is unclear. The last time the rapper mentioned her was in 2016 when he celebrated their 12th year of being together.
