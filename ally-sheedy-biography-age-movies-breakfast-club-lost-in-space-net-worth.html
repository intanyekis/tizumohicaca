BIOGRAPHIE D’ALLY SHEEDY | QUI EST ALLY SHEEDY?

Ally Sheedy (Alexandra Elizabeth Sheedy) is an American actress and author. She made her film debut in Bad Boys in 1983. She later became known as one of the Brat Pack cast members in the films The Breakfast Club (1985) and St. Elmo’s Fire (1985). Sheedy also starred in WarGames (1983) and Short Circuit (1986). She won the Independent Spirit Award for Best Female Lead for her performance in Lisa Cholodenko’s High Art (1998).

Ally attended Columbia Grammar & Preparatory School in New York and graduated in 1980. She then began dancing with American Ballet Theater at the age of six and planned to make it a career of her own. whole. Sheedy, however, gave up dancing in favor of full-time acting.
